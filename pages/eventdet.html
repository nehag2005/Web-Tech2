Cats Whiskers Cat Cafe, 8-9 Kirkgate, Leeds LS1 6BZ, UK.

The venue is just a short walk from the Leeds train station and easily accessible by bus, making it convenient. If you're arriving by car, there are several paid parking garages nearby. The Cats Whiskers Cat Cafe is known for its relaxed atmosphere and, of course, its adorable feline residents. Get ready to celebrate!

You can book your place by visiting your nearest Catbook booking office. Find the office closest to you using our Nearest Office page. Remember to bring the tickets you are provided with by these offices!

We want everyone to feel welcome and enjoy our cat-themed celebration! The venue has been selected with accessibility in mind, offering step-free entrances and accessible restroom facilities. If you have any specific accessibility needs or concerns, please reach out to us in advance through our Contact Us page. We'll do our best to accommodate your request so you can focus on the festivities and feline fun!
