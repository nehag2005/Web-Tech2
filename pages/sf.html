Scottish Fold
Hello there! I'm a Scottish Fold, a unique and charming breed of cat. My distinctive feature is my folded ears, which give me an adorable and distinctive appearance. I come in a variety of colors and patterns, including tabby, solid, bicolor, and more. I owe my origins to a single barn cat named Susie, who lived on a farm in Scotland in the 1960s. She had the natural genetic mutation that caused her ears to fold forward. Overall, I'm a delightful addition to any home, bringing joy, companionship, and plenty of adorable antics to brighten your day.
