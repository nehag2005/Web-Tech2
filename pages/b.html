Bengal
Hello! I'm a Bengal cat, a striking breed known for my exotic appearance and playful nature. My breed's origins can be traced back to the 1960s when a domestic cat was bred with a wild Asian Leopard Cat. I have a muscular build and a sleek body, perfect for agile movements and impressive leaps. My coat comes in various colors, including brown, silver, and snow, each with its unique allure. Overall, I'm a captivating companion for those who appreciate a touch of the wild combined with the warmth of a loving pet.
